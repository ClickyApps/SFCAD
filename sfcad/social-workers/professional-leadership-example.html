The following illustration may help:

I have taken part in several induction events in my service for new staff. I asked if I could make a contribution and I have been given the responsibility to explain what local resources are available in the area and how applications can be made to the most important ones. As a follow up exercise, I have compiled a resource on the local electronic shared drive. I have also mentored one of the new social work assistants, providing him with regular opportunities to talk through how he can get the best out of the job. I have really enjoyed this activity and it has given me food for thought about becoming a practice educator. As someone comfortable with the use of technology, I am regularly called upon in the office to help newer staff navigate the electronic recording system. I received some training at the beginning myself but since then I have mastered the process and I am happy to help others. Having identified a gap I took the initiative, and I am now going to be part of a new initiative trialling the use of tablets.
