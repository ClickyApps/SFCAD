About Social Workers Level

In the Social Work role practitioners practice effectively, exercising higher quality judgements, in situations of increasing complexity, risk, uncertainty and challenge than during their Assessed and Supported Year in Employment (ASYE). Through growing understanding they expect and anticipate, but do not pre-judge, the issues that may develop. They have greater confidence and independence (whilst accessing support when needed), and use their initiative to broaden their repertoire of responses; they have expertise in one or more areas of practice, be familiar with local resource networks and be recognised by peers as a source of reliable knowledge and advice.
